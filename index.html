About Us

Geo-Data Mobile Solutions is a Tanzanian, Pan-African Company aiming at complementing government efforts to serve the marginalized rural farmers to actively contribute to the African countries’ modern economy while raising their standard of living.

Our Strategies

We want to support rural farmers to formalize and titling their land and valuate their properties and link them to the appropriate stakeholders who in turn can do business with to raise their income and contribute to the modern economy.

Our Vision

To become the Africa’s most sought Integrated Land Use Data Hub which unlocks farmers’ potential to modern economy where; men, women and young people regardless of their residence will have reduced poverty, inequality and have access to resources for good their quality life.

We partner with central and local governments, Donors, Investors, Banks, Insurers, Pension Funds, Buyers, agro inputs dealers, food processors, Marketers, Land rights activists and local organizations to ensure that rural women and men living in extreme poverty have formalize their land they depend on. When families have secure rights and formalize their land and properties, they can invest in their land to sustainably increase their harvests and reap the benefits—improved nutrition, health, education, and improve their lives and their families. After formalizing and titling last Mile farmers we put all together in a single platform for them to meet and exchange for positive impact.

Beneficiaries

Direct rural women and men including youths farmers both small scale and medium who possess land and related properties to be formalized in Tanzania.

The project will directly employ over 500K employees in the first five years

The project will benefit over 100 government district GIS/ land surveyors
